Add a Profile Picture in SharePoint & Outlook

So that your photo shows up in either MS Outlook or the SharePoint site:

  1. Go to the waffle.
  2. Do you have the Delve app in your list of apps?
    • If Yes, select it, then go to Step 5.
    • If No, select the link, Explore all your apps at the bottom of your listed apps, then go to Step 3.
  3. Look to the right side of the screen and select the All apps link.
  4. Select the camera icon and upload your picture.
  5. Click Apply to save.
  6. Refresh your screen or check Delve or your email within a few minutes to see the change. MS Outlook (desktop) may take 24 hours to update.
  7. Done.
